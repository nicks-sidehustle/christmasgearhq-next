# ChristmasGearHQ Content Guide

## Content Pillars

1. **Tree Setup** - Stands, skirts, artificial trees, tree care
2. **Lighting** - Indoor, outdoor, smart lights, projectors
3. **Decorations** - Ornaments, stockings, wreaths, themed decor
4. **Outdoor Display** - Inflatables, yard decor, weatherproof items
5. **Organization** - Storage, protection, year-round maintenance

## Content Types

### Product Reviews
- Individual product deep-dives
- Emphasize: durability, ease of setup, value over seasons
- Length: 500-800 words

### Buying Guides
- Category roundups ("Best Tree Stands 2026")
- Include: quick picks, detailed reviews, size guides
- Length: 1500-2500 words
- Feature 5-10 products per guide

### Seasonal Blog Posts
- "How to" content (hanging lights safely, storing ornaments)
- Trend content (color schemes, themes)
- Length: 800-1200 words

## Tone & Voice

**Overall tone:** Warm, helpful, excited about the holidays but practical

**Do:**
- Acknowledge that setup can be stressful
- Emphasize family time and tradition
- Be honest about durability and value
- Include size/space guidance
- Mention safety where relevant (lights, outdoor)

**Don't:**
- Be preachy about "the real meaning of Christmas"
- Assume unlimited budgets
- Ignore renters/apartment dwellers
- Skip practical concerns (storage, setup time)

## SEO Guidelines

### Title Patterns
- Reviews: "[Product] Review: [Key Benefit] for Your Holiday"
- Guides: "Best [Category] 2026: [Number] Top Picks for Every Budget"
- Blog: "How to [Task] Without the Holiday Stress"

## Product Selection Criteria

- [ ] Rating: 4.0+ stars on Amazon
- [ ] Reviews: 100+ reviews (seasonal items may have less)
- [ ] Price: Ranges for all budget tiers
- [ ] Durability: Emphasis on multi-season use
- [ ] Availability: Check Oct-Nov availability especially

## Quality Gates

Before publishing:

1. [ ] Products actually relevant to Christmas/holidays
2. [ ] No summer/outdoor furniture sneaking in
3. [ ] Images are festive and appropriate
4. [ ] Safety notes where needed (electrical, ladder use)
5. [ ] Sizing/measurement info included

## Prohibited Content

- Generic home decor (unless holiday-specific)
- Religious-specific items (keep broadly Christmas-themed)
- Content from other GearHQ sites
- Anything with REPLACE_ still in it
